What prerequisites are needed for your courses?

Our courses cater to all levels. Beginners need basic computer skills, while advanced courses may require networking or programming knowledge. Check individual course pages for details.

Are certifications included with the courses?

Yes, most courses include preparation for industry-recognized certifications, such as CEH or CompTIA Security+. Completion certificates are also provided.

Can I access labs remotely?

Absolutely! Our labs are cloud-based, allowing secure remote access from anywhere with an internet connection.
